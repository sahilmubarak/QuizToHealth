What does Introvert mean?


Being an Introvert means that you like to focus more on your internal feelings, moods and thoughts rather than finding external stimulation.


Being an Introvert doesn't mean that you are shy or socially anxious, it just means that you prefer spending your time alone to spending it with a group of people.


In short, it means that you feel energized when alone and drained of energy when with many people.
