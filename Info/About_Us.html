About Us

QuizToHealth.com is a website dedicated to help spread awareness about Health, Fitness and Life by simplifying the learning process and making it enjoyable.
We try to keep our quizes short and simple, to make it easy for everyone to learn.

How we find topics for quizes.

To keep our quizes relavent and interesting we have to keep looking for new and interesting topics.
We do this by constantly looking for any new information available on the internet. If we find a topic that we think could help people in any way, we go through varoius steps of research and study to make a quiz on the topic.

How we make our Quizes

After a topic is selected, we try our best to find any available information that might be related to the topic. Once we collect the needed information we study and analyse it, then frame a number questions on it, as not all questions can be used for a quiz we group questions into sub-topics and make multiple quizes using the questions.

For quizes on topics related to health, we add information about the answer below each question to help interested users understand the topic.

To make information accessible, the links to the sources used to make the quiz are availabe at the end of each quiz.
