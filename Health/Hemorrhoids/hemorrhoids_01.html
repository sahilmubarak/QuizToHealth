What are Hemorrhoids?


Hemorrhoids commonly called Piles are swollen veins in the lowest part of your rectum and anus.


Sometimes, the walls of these blood vessels stretch so thin that the veins bulge and get irritated. hemorrhoids are one of the most common causes of rectal bleeding. They often go away on their own. Treatments can also help.


- WebMD
