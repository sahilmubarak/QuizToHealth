White Fungus


Know the answer for some important questions about the White Fungus, in simple points.

What is White Fungus?

  • White Fungus is a fungal infection, also known as invasive candidiasis.
  • It is caused by a group of yeasts called Candida.
  • It can affect the blood, heart, brain, eyes, bones, or other parts of the body.

    • Why is it called White Fungus?

  • Infected people are reported to have White patches on their tongue and areas of the mouth.
  • It is called White Fungus because of it's symptoms and not because of the actual color of the yeast.

    • Is it a new type of Fungus?

  • No, White Fungus or candidiasis in general is not a new Infection.
  • They are called Opportunistic infections.
  • It means that they only cause infection when there is an opportunity.
  • For example, in people with a weak immune system.

    • Is it related to Black Fungus?

  • Both White and Black Fungus are caused by fungi, but their severity and symptoms are different.
  • Black Fungus is caused by fungus Mucor and White Fungus is caused by candida.

    • Is White Fungus Deadlier than Black Fungus?

  • Experts say that White Fungus is DEADLIER than Black Fungus.
  • White Fungus infects vital organs like Lungs, Kidney, Brain and Genital organs, which makes it very dangerous.
  • This does not mean that Black Fungus isn't Deadly, it too can cause sevier illness and even death.

    • How does White Fungus infect the body?

  • Doctors say that the infection usualy starts from the Mouth or Genital region.
  • It then spreads to various organs of the body through the blood stream.

    • Is White Fungus contagious?

  • As of now White Fungus is not believed to be contagious in most cases, meaning that it cannot spread from person to person.
  • However, there are some species of Fungus that cause skin infections, and these infections can be contagious.

    • Who is at risk of getting White Fungus?

  • As said earlier, White Fungus is an Opportunistic infection. Meaning that it infects people with a weak Immune System.

    • People at most risk of getting infected are:

  • People taking immuno-suppressant medications.
  • People who have been taking steroids for a long time.
  • Or people with Diabetes, Cancer or any health condition that might weaken the immune system.

    • What are the Symptoms of White Fungus?

  • Infected people are being reported to have symptoms that are similar to that of Covid-19.

    • Which includes Symptoms like:

  • Dry Cough
  • Fever
  • Vomiting
  • Headache
  • White spots on tongue
  • Diarrhea
  • Reduced oxygen level

    • Since the symptoms of White Fungus are similar to those of Covid19, CT scan and X-Ray is being used for identifying the infection.

    What can you do to pervent getting a White Fungus infection?

    Experts say that the most important thing is to avoid getting infected by Covid-19.

    A White Fungus Infection can be prevented by following some precautions:

  • Only take steroids when necessary.
  • Keep your blood sugar level in check.
  • Avoid inhaling steam.
  • Use Antibiotics only when necessary.
  • Keep your surroundings clean.
  • Maintain a strong and healthy immune system.

    • Contact a doctor for clarification or more information.

    Should you be worried about the White Fungus?

  • Fortunately, White Fungus infections are extremely rare so most people have nothing to worry about.
  • But if you have any health condition which might have affected your immunity, you should follow the necessary precautions.
  • If you feel any symptoms of the infection, consult a doctor immediately.
